Welcome to the Concur Mobile Style Guide!

This style guide is provided to help Concur designers and engineers build consistant and engaging applications for Concur.

How to use this Style Guide

The Concur Mobile styleguide is broken up by platforms. Each platform has their own requirments for design and are unique to their eco-system. As more platforms are added to Concur we (the Mobile Design team) will add them to the style guide.

Each platform section consists of style, typography, color, icons, animation, layyout, and components. As seen in the navigation to the left.
