Helvetica Neue Font Family

Helvetica Neue is the default font in iOS, and the one we use in the Concur Mobile App for IOS.

Normal text is usually displayed in the Light face. Text that should appear more prominent is displayed in Medium face (eg. the title in Navigation Bar). Buttons often appear as simple colored text links.

For Adobe Photoshop comps/mockups Helvetica Neue fonts ships free on iOS platforms. For Adobe Photoshop on Microsoft Windows OS we recommend Helvetica Neue from Linotype.
