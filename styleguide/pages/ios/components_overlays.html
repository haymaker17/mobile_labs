Active Overlays

Active Overlays are a way of displaying more details when there's not enough space or it's not pertinent to show on the main screen. These are generally initiated by a tap on an icon or button.

Passive Overlays

Passive Overlays are a way means of notifying or alerting the user there's an action required or something has gone wrong. An example is when a form is submitted without a required field, a Passive Overlay may come up to alert the user to fill in the required field.
