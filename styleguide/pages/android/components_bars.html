Android Action Bar

The action bar is a dedicated piece of real estate at the top of each screen that is generally persistent throughout the app. We are adapting the new Android L style to keep the app design up to date.

This is the currect Concur Android action bar using white background and blue text color.

Specification

Navigation bar height : 24dp
Background color: #0066aa
Action bar height: 56dp
Background color: #0078c8
Text color: #ffffff
