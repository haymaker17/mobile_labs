Typeface

Roboto is the default font in Android, and the one we use in the Concur Mobile App for Android.

Normal text is usually displayed in the Light face. Text that should appear more prominent is displayed in Medium face (eg. the title in Navigation Bar). Buttons often appear as simple colored text links.
