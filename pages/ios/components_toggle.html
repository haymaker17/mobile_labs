Toggle

When multiple selections are required in a list view, the user first taps the "select" icon and the view is put in "select mode". Now the user may select one or multiple items by tapping on them and then use the contextual actions that appear on the bottom of the screen to act on those items.

Specs

Font, color, and Usage:

  • 'Checked' background fill: Concur Blue #0078C8
  • 'Unchecked' stroke: Medium Grey #A7B6BF
