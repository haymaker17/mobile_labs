Status Flags

Currently there are three different states where we use the triangle Status Flags. The flags appear on the top, right-hand corner of the cell they're related to. They are used to indicate the following:

Waiting to Upload

Specs

Color, and Usage:

  • Background fill: Dark Grey #7F8F97

Usage

'Waiting to Upload' status flags indicate that the file is pending an internet connection and has yet to be uploaded.

Error

Specs

Color, and Usage:

  • Background fill: Red #BF6767

Usage

'Error' status flags indicate that a correction is required. The user is unable to proceed to the next screen until an adjustment is made to the flagged component.

Warning

Specs

Color, and Usage:

  • Background fill: Yellow #F4A900

Usage

'Warning' status flags indicate that an additional field has been added or a correction needs the users attention.
