Activity Indicator

An activity indicator shows that a task or process is progressing (shown to the right with text labels). There are two types of activity indicators, One is used for when a portion of the content is loading and the other is used when the entire page needs to be refreshed.

Specs

Color and Usage:

  • Background fill: White #FFFFFF
  • Activity Indicator: 27 tall
  • Activity Indicator: 27 wide
