Welcome to the Concur Style Guide!

This style guide is provided to help Concur designers and engineers build consistent and engaging applications for Concur.

How to use this Style Guide

The Concur style guide is broken up by platforms. Each platform has their own requirements for design and are unique to their eco-system. As more platforms are added to Concur we (the Design team) will add them to the style guide.

Each platform section consists of style, typography, color, icons, animation, layyout, and components. As seen in the navigation to the left.
